Sales Insights

Total sales achieved $10.9M with a strong average order value of $1,149.

Monthly sales remained stable with peaks in June and October 2023.

Table, Sofa, Lipstick, Laptop, and Rice are the top contributors to revenue (each > $1M).

Leading regions: Bali, Jakarta, and Bandung, all above $2.18M.

Customer segments contribute almost evenly: Wholesale (33.76%), Retail (33.32%), and Online (32.92%).

Order Insights

Order volume shows consistent performance month to month.

Top-ordered products: Lipstick, Phone, Milk, Laptop, and Rice.

Payment method usage is balanced across Bank Transfer, E-Wallet, Credit Card, and Cash.

Orders are distributed evenly across customer segments: Wholesale, Retail, and Online.
